Virat Kohli-The Indian Cricketer

The most notable and eminent player from the Indian team.

Virat Kohli is currently the most renowned cricketer all over the world from India.He is the most followed sports person from India.

He was born on 5th of November in Delhi into a Punjabi-Hindu family to the couple Premnath Kohli,a criminal lawyer and Saroj Kohli.He started his career with the debut into Delhi Domestic cricket at the very young age of just 18 years old.Virat has joined into the IPL RCB franchise in the year 2008. He lead the Indian cricket team in all three formats over the period 2013-2022,after MS Dhoni.He has an unconditional fanbase all around the globe.

The lovely cricket couple

Virat met the talented bollywood actress Anushka Sharma in the shampoo advertisement shooting and they started dating each other.The couple tied the knot on December 11th 2017 in Italy.Virat and Anushka are parents for 2 kids,daughter Vamika Kohli who was born on January 11 2021 and a son Akaay Kohli who was born on Febraury 15 2024.
